General structure:

Class Cell:
	variables:	list of neighbors (Cells)
			tuple of up/down - left/right position (int,int)
			tuple with states (old/new)
			pointer to state


	methods:	stay_alive(list of neighbors, state) -> True/False


main:
	variables:	2D array with Cells (index = position)



	methods:	initialize_neighbors (2d list with Cells)
			update_fkt(2d list with Cells)
			output()
